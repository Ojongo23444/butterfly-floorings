Why Epoxy Your Garage Floor?

GTA Garage Floors

A professional garage floor epoxy coating system transforms that worn concrete into a durable, chemical-resistant, showroom-quality surface. Our Process

How We Install
Garage Epoxy in the GTA

01
Surface Preparation

Diamond grinding removes all contamination, opens the concrete pores, and creates the mechanical profile required for a chemical bond. This step is what separates a lasting job from a peeling failure.

02
Epoxy Base Coat

100% solids epoxy primer is applied and penetrates the prepared concrete, creating a permanent chemical bond. Any cracks or joints are filled and reinforced at this stage.

03
Broadcast & Build

Decorative flakes or colour chips are broadcast into the wet epoxy. For solid colour systems a second epoxy build coat is applied for additional thickness and durability.

04
Polyaspartic Topcoat

A UV-stable polyaspartic topcoat seals the system, prevents yellowing, and provides the scratch and abrasion resistance that makes our floors last for decades — not years.

Common Questions

Garage Floor Epoxy FAQs
Greater Toronto Area

How much does garage floor epoxy coating cost in the GTA?
Garage floor epoxy coating in the GTA typically costs between $2,500 and $5,500 for a standard two-car garage (400–600 sq ft), depending on floor condition, the system chosen, and any crack repairs required. We provide free on-site quotes with no obligation — contact us for an accurate price.
How long does epoxy flooring last?
A professionally installed epoxy system with a polyaspartic topcoat will last 15–25 years under normal residential use, and 8–15 years in high-traffic commercial environments. DIY kits from hardware stores typically last 2–5 years because they use water-based formulations with far lower solids content.
How long does epoxy floor installation take?
Most residential garage epoxy jobs across the GTA are completed in one day. You can return to light foot traffic within 12–24 hours and drive on the floor within 48–72 hours. Larger multi-bay projects vary based on square footage.
Will garage floor epoxy peel in a GTA winter?
This is the most common concern we hear — and it's valid. Cheap DIY kits do peel in Ontario winters. Professionally installed epoxy with correct diamond grinding, a 100% solids base coat, and a quality polyaspartic topcoat handles GTA freeze-thaw cycles extremely well. Proper surface preparation is what makes the difference.
Which GTA cities do you install garage floor epoxy in?
We install garage floor epoxy across the entire GTA including Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Oakville, Burlington, Etobicoke, Vaughan, Markham, and Scarborough. See our epoxy flooring Toronto and epoxy flooring Mississauga pages for city-specific information.
