What Is Epoxy Flooring?

Durable Epoxy Floors
Built for Mississauga

Epoxy flooring is a high-performance coating system applied directly to concrete. It bonds chemically with the substrate to create a surface that is far harder, more durable, and more attractive than bare concrete alone. Butterfly Floorings installs epoxy systems across Mississauga, Brampton, Toronto, and all surrounding GTA cities.

Our epoxy systems are 3–5x thicker than big-box store DIY kits, use 100% solids or high-solids epoxy resins, and are installed by trained professionals who understand Mississauga's climate — including freeze-thaw conditions that cause inferior coatings to peel and fail.

  • Resists oil, chemicals, salts, and automotive fluids
  • Seamless, non-porous surface — easy to clean
  • Available in dozens of colours, flakes, and metallic finishes
  • Slip-resistant topcoat options for safety compliance
  • Installed in as little as 1–2 days with fast cure times

Our Process

How We Install
Epoxy Flooring in Mississauga

01
Surface Preparation

Diamond grinding removes all contamination, opens the concrete pores, and creates the mechanical profile required for a chemical bond. This step is what separates a lasting job from a peeling failure.

02
Epoxy Base Coat

100% solids epoxy primer is applied and penetrates the prepared concrete, creating a permanent chemical bond. Any cracks or joints are filled and reinforced at this stage.

03
Broadcast & Build

Decorative flakes or colour chips are broadcast into the wet epoxy. For solid colour systems a second epoxy build coat is applied for additional thickness and durability.

04
Polyaspartic Topcoat

A UV-stable polyaspartic topcoat seals the system, prevents yellowing, and provides the scratch and abrasion resistance that makes our floors last for decades — not years.

Common Questions

Epoxy Flooring FAQs
Mississauga & GTA

How much does epoxy garage flooring cost in Mississauga?
Epoxy garage flooring in Mississauga typically costs between $2,500 and $5,500 for a standard two-car garage (400–600 sq ft), depending on floor condition, system chosen, and any crack repairs required. We provide free on-site quotes — contact us for an accurate price for your specific garage.
How long does epoxy flooring last?
A professionally installed epoxy system with a polyaspartic topcoat will last 15–25 years under normal residential use, and 8–15 years in high-traffic commercial environments. DIY kits from hardware stores typically last 2–5 years because they use water-based formulations with far lower solids content.
How long does epoxy floor installation take?
Most residential garage epoxy jobs in Mississauga are completed in one day. You can typically return to light foot traffic within 12–24 hours and drive on the floor within 48–72 hours. Commercial and industrial projects vary based on square footage.
Does epoxy flooring work in cold weather? Will it peel in a Mississauga winter?
Professionally installed epoxy with proper surface preparation and a quality topcoat handles Ontario winters well. The key is correct installation — the concrete must be clean, dry, and properly profiled before application. Our polyaspartic topcoats are specifically formulated for the freeze-thaw cycles of the GTA climate.
Do you serve areas outside Mississauga?
Yes — we serve the entire Greater Toronto Area including Toronto, Brampton, Oakville, Burlington, Etobicoke, Vaughan, Markham, and Scarborough. Contact us to confirm availability for your specific location.
